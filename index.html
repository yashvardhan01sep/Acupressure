🧠 What is Acupressure?

Acupressure is a traditional healing method based on applying pressure to specific points on the body to relieve pain and promote wellness. It’s similar to acupuncture but without needles — using fingers or tools instead.

💡 Benefits of Acupressure

Acupressure is known for its numerous health benefits, including:

  • Relieves stress, anxiety, and fatigue
  • Helps with joint pain, headaches, and back pain
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Boosts energy flow (Chi/Prana)
  • No side effects – completely natural
