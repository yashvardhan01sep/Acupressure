🔹 About Acupressure

Acupressure is a centuries-old healing method that involves applying gentle pressure to specific points on the body. These points, often located along energy pathways called meridians, help release blocked energy, reduce pain, and restore balance. Acupressure is safe, non-invasive, and effective for managing stress, fatigue, body aches, and even emotional tension.

🔸 How Acupressure Works

By stimulating pressure points, acupressure encourages the flow of vital energy (Qi) and activates the body’s natural self-healing abilities. It is commonly used for headaches, back pain, digestive issues, and improving sleep.
